PaintXD

Welcome to PaintXD, the ultimate drawing tool designed to inspire your creativity and bring your imagination to life. It's important to remember that true artistry lies within the artist, not the tools they use. PaintXD embraces simplicity while providing the essential features of drawing apps.

Features

  • Pencil:With our pencil tool, you can sketch, doodle, and draw with precision. Create intricate lines and add intricate details to your artwork.
  • Color Picker: Choose from an extensive color palette or pick custom colors to add depth and vibrancy to your drawings.
  • Eraser: Correct mistakes and make adjustments effortlessly with the eraser tool.
  • Resolution Adjustment: Adjust the resolution of your canvas to suit your needs(max 1000px X 1000px).
  • Import Images: Expand your creative possibilities by importing images directly into PaintXD.
  • Export and Download: Once you're satisfied with your creation, PaintXD lets you export and save your artwork.
